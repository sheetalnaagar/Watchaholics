WHO ARE WE?

WATCHAHOLICS Registered under UAE Economy department under Trade license number:796675000
Watchaholics was founded by Manik Malhotra Entrepreneur in the United Arab Emirates, under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Farrukh Establishment (Dubai SME). The concept of Luxury watches came to life in 1992 and established his own luxury watches company in 2018. The entity trades in authentic high-end Swiss Made watches andpersonal luxury goods such as Hermes, Vertu, and branded jewelry.Feel personalized experience in our boutique. Friendly environment to feel like home and displaying more than 50 brands. At the core of Watchaholics is a team of watch enthusiasts who love what they do. Not only do we have a passion for watches, we also strive to make each and every one of our customers happy. From watchmakers to marketing specialists to sales consultants to tech engineers and everything in between, we have experts in all areas to ensure that Watchaholics delivers a topnotch customer experience, every time.
